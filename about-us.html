The Full Story

We are a Dubai based Interior Design, Real Estate and Construction Company, specialising in: residential, commercial and retail properties within the UAE.

Established in 2018 by seasoned professionals, we are proud to be a recognized and respected name within the UAE real estate community. Our large portfolio offers our discerning clients the opportunity to invest in one of the world’s fastest growing property markets.

Thom & Gery has been working as a team to provide our clients with the very best in real estate services. Innovation is key to every area of our business, which is built on exceeding our clients’ expectations. Whether you are looking to purchase, sell or relocate, our office can assist you with all of your real estate needs. We are in the business of helping people materialise their real estate dreams.

Our Values

Our Misson

Through a network of qualified sellers and purchasers, our mission is to match people with the right property.

We strive to make property investment decisions in your life, as stress-free as possible.

Our Vision

Our vision is to lead the local real estate market in providing property solutions.

Our professionalism and performance will result in maintaining market share and our commitment to customer service will ensure we earn continued business from the people we deal with.

Our Goals

Our goal is to build relationships for a lifetime – not a transaction.

You can count on us to support you not just before and during your purchasing or selling experience, but we will remain a resource and support to you after, also.

Our commitment to you is that we will lend to you the expertise that will demystify the buying experience of Dubai real estate with the goal of making it an enjoyable one!
